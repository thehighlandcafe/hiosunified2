Check In Help


Need help on how to check in to one of our branches? Kindly follow the steps below:
  1. First, click on the button that says "WorstEastern" in the navigation drawer, then click on the checkin button.

  2. Scroll down and fill in all the details asked in the form using the on-screen keyboard

  3. Click SUBMIT to finish checking in

  4. Please go back to the navigation drawer and tap "Home" to go home, or, alternatively, open another activity.
